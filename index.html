Network Rigging Services

Network Rigging Services Australia Pty Ltd specialize in all aspects of communications rigging.

The Directors of Network Rigging Services have 40 years combined experience in communications and broadcast rigging. During the past ten years we have worked with Kordia NZ, Television New Zealand, Skycommunications, Downers, Abigroup, Ericsson, Theiss and Servicestream.

We currently have a workforce of 12 riggers based in Victoria and have spent the last three years upgrading the Telstra Network and more recently assisting Theiss and Servicestream with their Vodafone EJV contracts.

“Our riggers have the appropriate certificates and training, our vehicles are equipped with Radmans, Sitemasters, tools and rescue equipment to complete on site works.”

Tony Sharp, Director

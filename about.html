About

Network Rigging Services Australia Pty Ltd specialize in all aspects of communications rigging.

The Directors of Network Rigging Services have 40 years combined experience in communications and broadcast rigging. During the past ten years we have worked with Kordia NZ, Television New Zealand, Skycommunications, Downers, Abigroup, Ericsson, Theiss and Servicestream.

We currently have a workforce of 12 riggers based in Victoria and have spent the last three years upgrading the Telstra Network and more recently assisting Theiss and Servicestream with their Vodafone EJV contracts.

Our riggers have the appropriate certificates and training, and our vehicles are equipped with Radmans, Sitemasters, tools and rescue equipment to complete on site works

Network Rigging Services is equipped to perform a wide range of works including, Installation and Maintenance of Cellular Base Stations, Digital Microwave Radio Links, Satellite Base Stations, Teamtalk and Mobile Radio Sites, High Frequency Radio Installations, Rope Access/Abseiling Work and Tower Modifications.

Our services can be engaged through a competitive hourly rate or as quoted works.

Network Rigging Services has a comprehensive health and safety policy, which can be viewed on request.

If we can be of assistance to you, or if you require further information please contact us anytime.
